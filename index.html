Troy Adam Gordon
CEO / Executive Producer

Zaqir has always considered himself
to be an interpreter, a guide in a
wasteland of overly complicated
plotlines and run-on
sentences that can be
ideally handled with the
perfect one-liner and a
casual wink. Add a keyboard,
a few years of grad school,
and enough April Wine
to round out the night,
and you’ve got yourself
the Head Writer of
TAG Creative.
The four things that
matter most to Zaqir
are consistency,
repetition, repetition,
and consistency.
Superpowers may
include illiteracy.

If Troy can leave you with one thing, it’s
that he wants every ad that he makes
to be fun enough that you watch it
twice. Once when you first see
it, and again four minutes later
when you’re showing it to your
buddy. After working as an
international documentary
filmmaker, Troy began
making born-digital video
copy and started TAG
Creative to create an
environment where MAD
Magazine would say
“Calm down!”
Troy loves anime, and
would love to talk
to you about it.
Like, a lot.
And yes, the
beard is sentient.
